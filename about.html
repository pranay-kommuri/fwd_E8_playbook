Playbook: Celebrating Traditional Indian Sports
From the high-energy kabaddi arenas to the timeless charm of kho-kho, we delve into the roots of India’s sporting heritage. Playbook is dedicated to preserving these age-old traditions while inspiring a new generation to embrace their rich legacy. Through insightful articles, player stories, and event coverage, we honor the skills, teamwork, and grit that define Indian sports. At Playbook, we believe that these sports are more than games—they are a reflection of India’s diverse heritage and unyielding spirit. Join us in celebrating the traditions that inspire and unite us. Let’s revive, cherish, and play the games that define India’s soul!
